Intro

I'm passionate about working smart, doing what's right, and making the world a better place.

I’m also an amateur jazz guitarist, and much like jazz I’m at my best when I’m adapting to change, finding harmony in chaos, and collaborating with technically skilled and creative people. PMP, CSM, and CSPO certified, as well as currently undertaking a Master’s Degree in Computer Science, I am committed to continuous learning and improvement, always seeking to expand my knowledge and skills in this dynamic field. With over 3 years’ experience leading cross functional teams to design, develop and deliver mission-focused programming and technology products, I bring a unique blend of business savvy and technical expertise. I’m always eager to connect with like-minded professionals who share a passion for innovation and impact.

Work

As a Program Manager at DoSomething.org (2022–Present), I lead initiatives empowering youth activism through technology, including developing a natural language-to-SQL application, engineering data visualizations (d3, Leaflet), and driving cross-functional teams to deliver award-winning projects. I spearhead organizational transformations, implement enterprise software solutions, and support agile technical processes for a novel web platform. Previously, as a Project Testing and Training Coordinator at Fast Enterprises (2021–2022), I managed GovTech implementations with less than 1% defect rates, trained 400+ users, and streamlined stakeholder communication. Earlier, at Youth Job Center (2021), I optimized program workflows via Salesforce and fostered cross-organizational collaboration. My career combines technical expertise in software development, data visualization, and CRM systems with a proven ability to lead teams, deliver scalable solutions, and drive impactful social change.

Education

Currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science from the University of Colorado Boulder with a Graduate Certificate in Generative AI, I am a skilled professional with skills in software development, machine learning, AI, and more. Combined with my undergraduate degree in Organizational Change from Northwestern University, I bring a unique blend of technical knowledge and business savvy.
